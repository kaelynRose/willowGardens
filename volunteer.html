Volunteer Opportunities

Teams are a great way to get to know other gardeners and volunteer on your own schedule.

New Gardener Mentor

Our mentors are responsible for providing guidance on topics such as soil preparation, plant selection, and pest management, as well as helping new gardeners develop their own gardening skills and knowledge.

Plot Maintenance

Plot maintenance volunteers ensure that the garden plots are well maintained, organized, and in good condition for planting and havesting throughout the growing season.

Water Maintenance

Water maintenance volunteers ensure that the garden has a reliable and efficient water supply system, monitor the water usage, and maintain any irrigation equipment.

Equipment Mainenance

Equipment maintenance involves overseeing the maintenance and repair of all the community garden tools and equipment so they are in good working condition and readily available for use by the gardeners.

About Willow Gardens

Westfield, WI, wants Willow Gardens to be an entry point for all community members to participate in gardening. We provide outdoor recreational activities and a fun and friendly environment for people of all ages, incomes, and cultures to work the land together and share their gardening knowledge and ideas.

Join Our Garden

Gardening is a great way to grow your own food and positively impact your household food security. When you purchase a plot you receive:
•Land to rent, starter seeds, and on-site water.
•Community compost, garden tools, and wood chips when available.
•Measured, marked, and monitored plots.
•Mowed grass borders to prevent rodent habitat.
•Teaching materials and advice from our community mentors.

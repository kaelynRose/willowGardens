Community Garden Information

•Water is provided on site throughout the season.
•Garden tools are available for everyone to use.
•Free wood chips are provided gardener use.
•We offer hay and row cover for sale on our application.

Individual Plot Information

•Garden plots measure approximately 20 feet by 20 feet (400 square feet).
•Maximum number of plots per gardener is 4.
•Only organic gardening methods are allowed.
•We do not till our gardens so gardeners can plant perennials and leave up fences throughout the year.

Plot Cost

Plot cost can vary between $15 and $78 per plot/year, depending on your income level and size of your household/garden family.
